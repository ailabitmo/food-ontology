Abstract

Food Ontology is an ontology created for food and food products particularly which is being developed for MneMojno project to structure its knowledge base of food products and to interlink with other datasets (such as DBPedia) in future. The motivation for building a new ontology is the lack of reusable, non-toy ontology for food product domain. The ontology extends the most powerful ontology for describing all of the details of products and services GoodsRelations ontology, here in after referred to as the core ontology.
